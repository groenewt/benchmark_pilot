üìñ Methodology

Benchmark Configuration:

  • Number of Chunks: 30
  • Questions per Chunk: 20
  • Search Depth (TOP_K): 10
  • Generation Model: llama3.1:8b-instruct-q8_0

Question Types:

  • Short: Simple, direct questions under 10 words
  • Long: Detailed, comprehensive questions requiring full context
  • Direct: Questions about explicitly stated information
  • Implied: Questions requiring inference and contextual understanding
  • Unclear: Vague or ambiguous questions about general topics

Evaluation Metric:

Accuracy is measured as the percentage of questions where the correct source chunk appears in the TOP_K vector search results.
